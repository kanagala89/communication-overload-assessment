ℹ️ About This Assessment

🎯 Purpose

This assessment helps you identify communication overload patterns and provides personalized recommendations to improve your digital wellbeing and productivity.

🔬 Methodology

Our questions are based on validated research in workplace communication, digital wellbeing, and productivity studies. The scoring algorithm considers multiple factors including volume, urgency, and impact on focus.

🔒 Privacy

  • All responses are processed locally in your browser
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Results are not stored or shared
  • Take the assessment anonymously

📝 Instructions

  • Answer all questions honestly based on a typical work week
  • Consider your overall patterns, not just recent experiences
  • Results are most accurate when you reflect on genuine work habits
  • Take time to consider each question carefully

🏢 For Organizations

This tool can be used as part of workplace wellbeing initiatives. Consider using aggregate insights (without individual data) to inform communication policies and training programs.
