Communication Overload: Why It Matters

In today’s digital workplace, professionals are inundated with messages and meetings—often at the expense of their most important work. Understanding the scale and impact of communication overload is the first step toward reclaiming your time and wellbeing.

Key Statistics

  • 121 emails per day: The average office worker receives 121 emails daily, contributing to stress, interruptions, and lost productivity.
    Source: Brosix, 2023
  • 88% of the workweek spent communicating: Most knowledge workers spend up to 88% of their week on emails, chats, meetings, and calls, leaving little time for focused work.
    Sources: Brosix, 2023, Quantified AI, 2023
  • 60% experience digital communication burnout: A majority of employees report that communication overload negatively impacts their wellbeing and job satisfaction.
    Source: Brosix, 2023
